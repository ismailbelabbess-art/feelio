Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Mewonen is designed with privacy at its core. We collect absolutely no personal data. Everything you draw, write, or say remains exclusively on your device. No information is transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service.

How Your Data is Stored

Mewonen uses your browser's localStorage to save your mood history, drawings, and preferences. This data never leaves your device. You can delete it at any time from Settings.

Your Rights

Under global privacy laws including GDPR (European Union), CCPA (California), LGPD (Brazil), and PIPEDA (Canada), you have the right to: Access your data, Export your data, Delete your data, Withdraw consent. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise these rights directly through the Settings menu at any time.

Cookies & Tracking

Mewonen uses zero cookies, zero trackers, zero analytics, and zero advertising. We have no way to track you because we collect nothing.
